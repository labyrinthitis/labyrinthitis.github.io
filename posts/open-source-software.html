open source software

December 12, 2022

As a layman, I agree with the advocacy toward (Free and) Open Source Software, (F)OSS as an acronym.

A gentle way to get into the OSS world is by contributing to your favorite project's open repository by focusing on something small like an editorial correction. I did so this past week: I made a one-word correction on the Next.js repository. Seemingly excessive and daunting, it's a good starter.
